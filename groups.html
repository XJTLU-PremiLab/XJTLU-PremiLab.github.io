Math Problem Solving

PremiLab-Math is a sub-group of PremiLab at XJTLU, focusing on leveraging LLMs to solve mathematical problems, including Math Word Problems, Geometry Problems and Table Math Word Problems. We also investigate the interpretability of LLMs in math problem solving. Our group page is at PremiLab-Math.

3D Scene

The research group focuses on advancing 3D scene understanding, particularly in zero-shot learning for 3D object recognition. Their recent work explores open-pose 3D zero-shot classification, developing new benchmarks and methods to recognize objects in arbitrary orientations using techniques like angle refinement and diffusion-based knowledge transfer.
